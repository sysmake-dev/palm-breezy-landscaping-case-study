## SERVICES SECTION

Palm Breezy Landscaping offers reliable residential landscaping services designed to keep your property clean, healthy, and visually appealing throughout the year.

**FIVE SERVICE CARDS**

1. Routine Lawn Maintenance
   Regular lawn care including mowing, edging, trimming, and property cleanup to keep your yard consistently maintained.

2. Landscape Maintenace
   Ongoing care for shrubs, hedges, and landscape beds to keep your plants healthy and visually appealing.

3. Palm Tree Services
   Specialized trimming and care for palm trees and tropical plants commonly found in Florida landscapes.

4. Property Cleanup
   Seasonal and storm cleanup services that restore yards affected by overgrowth, debris, or severe weather.

5. Installation Services
   Professional installation of sod, landscape beds, and decorative plant layouts to improve your outdoor space.
