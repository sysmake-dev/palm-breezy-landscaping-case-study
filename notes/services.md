# SERVICES

## SERVICE LIST

### Routine Lawn Maintenance

Most residential clients will schedule weekly or bi-weekly.

#### Lawn Mowing

- Professional mowing for residential lawns.
- Weekly or bi-weekly service options.

#### Edging

- Clean edging along sidewalks, driveways, and landscape beds.

#### String Trimming

- Trimming around trees, fences, and hard-to-reach areas.

#### Blowing and Cleanup

- Clearing grass clippings and debris from patios, sidewalks, and driveways.

### Landscape Maintenance

#### Hedge and Shrub Trimming

- Shaping and maintaining hedges and ornamental shrubs.

#### Garden Bed Maintenance

- Removing weeds from landscape beds.
- Light soil grooming and cleanup.

#### Mulch Installation

- Installing fresh mulch in garden beds.
- Helps retain moisture and improve appearance.

### Florida-Specific Services

#### Palm Tree Trimming

- Removal of dead or hanging palm fronds.
- Removal of seed pods and debris.
- Improving safety and appearance.

#### Tropical Plant Care

- Maintenance of hibiscus, crotons, and other common Florida / tropical plants.

### Property Cleanup Services

#### Yard Cleanup

- Clearing overgrown grass.
- Removing debris and organic buildup.
- Restoring the property's appearance.

#### Storm Cleanup

- Removal of fallen branches.
- Clearing storm debris.
- Restoring yard condition.

### Installation Services

#### Sod Installation

- Installing fresh sod to repair damaged or dead lawn areas.

#### Landscape Bed Installation

- Creating or redesigning landscape beds.
- Planting shrubs or decorative plants.

### Service Add-Ons

#### Leaf Removal

- Seasonal cleanup of accumulated leaves.

#### Debris Hauling

- Removal and disposal of yard waste.

#### Mulch Refresh
